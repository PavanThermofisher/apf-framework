Evolving Beyond Traditional Models

The traditional "People, Processes, Tools" framework served well in the era of purely human development teams. As we enter an age where AI agents are genuine development partners, our frameworks must evolve to reflect this collaborative reality.

Traditional Model

  • People - Human expertise
  • Processes - Workflows
  • Tools - Technology stack

Governance in the Age of AI Agents

The integration of AI agents introduces new governance requirements that organizations must address:

Ethical Oversight

  • Bias detection and fairness auditing
  • Transparency requirements
  • Human accountability chains
  • Privacy protection safeguards

Quality Assurance

  • AI output validation processes
  • Failure mode planning
  • Performance monitoring
  • Rollback mechanisms

Risk Management

  • Dependency management strategies
  • Enhanced security protocols
  • Compliance integration
  • Intellectual property policies
